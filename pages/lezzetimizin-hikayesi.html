Our Flavour Story

Cradle of Natural Beauty and Flavours

Erzincan is a hidden treasure nestled in the unspoilt lands of Eastern Anatolia, in the east of Turkey.

With its snow-capped mountains, fertile plains, and deep valleys, this unique geography offers visitors both a visual feast and gifts the world of gastronomy with distinctive tastes.

These fertile lands, nourished by the Euphrates River, have been home to many civilisations throughout history, blending cultural richness with natural beauty.

Highland Pastures: The Source of Natural Riches

The Erzincan highlands, stretching between the Munzur and Kesis Mountains, are among the region's most valuable natural treasures.

Highlands such as Ergan, Cayirli, and Otlukbeli are adorned with colourful flowers in spring and transform into lush green carpets in summer.

The endemic plants and various herbs that grow in these highlands provide natural nourishment for the animals, contributing to the foundation of the region's gastronomy.

Sheep and goats that graze freely in these high-altitude pastures not only produce quality milk but also lie at the heart of the traditional lifestyle of the local people.

Erzincan Tulum Cheese: The Pride of the Highlands

Erzincan tulum cheese, one of Turkey's geographically indicated products, is the most valuable gastronomic heritage of these lands.

Made from the milk of herds that ascend to the highlands in May and June, this cheese stands out with its distinctive aroma and characteristic texture.

Prepared using traditional methods that have been practised for centuries, tulum cheese undergoes natural fermentation while ripening inside goatskin bags, deriving its unique flavour from this process.

Distinguished by its balanced salt content, creamy texture, and non-crumbling structure, this cheese is both an indispensable part of the table and a nationally recognised symbol of the city.

In Pursuit of Natural, Additive-Free Flavours

The Erzincan highlands are an ideal destination for those who wish to escape the cosmopolitan city life and find peace in the arms of nature.

A journey to this region offers a holistic experience that delights not only your eyes but also your palate.

During your highland visits, where you will encounter the hospitality of the local people, you can witness the production processes of handmade products that are part of the cultural heritage.

Every moment spent in the Erzincan highlands will remind you that a life intertwined with nature, far from the chaos of modern life, is possible, and will leave you with unforgettable memories.

A highland holiday enriched with activities such as nature walks, photography, and local cuisine workshops offers a unique opportunity to rejuvenate your body and soul.
