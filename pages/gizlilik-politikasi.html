Privacy Policy

At Ozum'le, protecting the privacy of our users and processing their personal data securely is of utmost importance to us.

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our website and contact us through our corporate WhatsApp line.

Information Collected

We do not collect personal information directly through our website.

However, if our users contact us through our corporate WhatsApp line, we may collect the following personal data:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your phone number
  • Other information shared via WhatsApp (text messages, images, documents, etc.)

Purposes of Using Personal Data

We use the personal data collected through our corporate WhatsApp line for the following purposes:

  • Responding to user requests and questions
  • Providing our services and increasing user satisfaction
  • Complying with legal obligations

Security of Personal Data

Personal data obtained through our corporate WhatsApp line is securely protected and accessible only by our authorised employees.

Thanks to WhatsApp's end-to-end encryption technology, your messages cannot be viewed by third parties.

Sharing of Personal Data

We share your personal data obtained via WhatsApp with third parties only in the following cases:

  • As required by legal obligations
  • With the user's consent

User Rights

As users, you have the following rights:

  • Requesting access to your personal data
  • Requesting correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Requesting deletion of your personal data (except for legal obligations)
  • Restricting the processing of your data

Changes to the Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time due to changes in laws and our services.

You can see the last update date of the policy on this page.
